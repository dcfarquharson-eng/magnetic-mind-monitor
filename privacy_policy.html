Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to The Magnetic Mind Institute. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website or the "Magnet: Power of Attraction" mobile application.

2. Data We Collect

To provide you with the best experience, we collect the following types of information when you use our app:

  • Contact Information: We collect your Name and Email Address when you create an account to personalize your experience and for account recovery.
  • Identifiers: We generate and collect a User ID to link your data to your account securely.
  • Purchases: We collect Purchase History to manage your auto-renewing premium subscriptions. Payment processing is handled securely by Apple; we do not store your credit card information directly.

The app also stores your daily manifestations and goals locally on your device and securely on our cloud servers to sync across your devices.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the services offered within the app, such as syncing your manifestation journal across devices, managing your premium subscription, and providing daily encouragement.

4. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We use industry-standard security measures to ensure your data is protected against unauthorized access.

5. Data Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data at any time. You can initiate account deletion directly within the "Magnet" mobile app settings menu, which will permanently remove your account, contact information, identifiers, and all associated journal data from our servers.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the data we collect, please contact us at admin@themagneticmind.institute.

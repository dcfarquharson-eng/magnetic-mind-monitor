Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 13, 2026

The Magnetic Mind Institute ("we", "our", or "us") is deeply committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have complete control over your personal development journey, especially when using our Digital Planner mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Our core philosophy is absolute privacy for your goals and aspirations. The Digital Planner app operates fundamentally as a local-first application.

2. Microphone Access (RECORD_AUDIO)

The Digital Planner app requests access to your device's microphone strictly to enable the "C-Type Goal" Voice Recording feature. This allows you to record your manifestations in your own voice for playback to impact your subconscious mind.

  • Local Storage Only: All audio recorded through the microphone is stored solely on your local device's database (SQLite).
  • No Cloud Sync: We do NOT transmit, upload, or sync your voice recordings to any external server, cloud database, or third-party service.
  • If you uninstall the app or log in from a different device, your recordings will not follow you.

3. Personal Data & Cloud Storage

The app utilizes a secure backend solely for authentication (account login) and storing non-sensitive streak synchronization to ensure your progress spans across devices. No highly personal text regarding your C-Goals is transmitted.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date".

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at dcfarquharson@gmail.com.
